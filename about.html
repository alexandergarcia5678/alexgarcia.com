About Me and My Work

Welcome!

My name is Alex Garcia. I am a Graphic Designer and a BFA candidate at The University of Louisville. My services range from poster and editorial design to user experience and web design. I find that I am my most recurrent client. I do many personal projects when something peaks my interest so that I can explore and grow my skills all that I can. I use this time working for myself to get out all my more experimental design ideas so that my artist ego doesn't get in the way of helping clients with their problem. When I work for clients I am working for them, not for my own portfolio.
